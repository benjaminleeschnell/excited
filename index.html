DMV Entertainment Day

Welcome Home!

This is where we go to the airport, find someone who is waiting for a friend or relative, and ask if we can help welcome them home. Their names get written on posterboards and they’re given a huge reception (and balloons and flowers) when they get off the plane.

Free Relationship Services

We go to a college dorm and set up a booth. We offer the following services for free: Writing love letters, breaking up with people, research (stalking) and making that first phone call.
